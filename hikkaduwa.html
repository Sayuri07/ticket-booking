Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery and Rescue Centre

Dedicated to preserving sea turtles



Hikkaduwa turtle hatchery is located on the beach roughly 2km north of Hikkaduwa. A small and private research center that aims to protect the endangered marine turtles. The hatchery offers a selective display of turtle eggs and exhibits the progressive development of turtles.


There are some spots where you can also get an oppurtunity to release baby turtles into the vast ocean for an additional fee.The best time to visit here and see turtle is March - April.

The facilities of the sea turtle hatchery and rescue centre are all state-of-the-art, complete with water tanks to care for new born turtless, along with adult turtle species that have been rescued from the sea due to injuries.



Open Daily: 09.00 a.m - 06.00 p.m

Entrance Fee: 500 LKR

Visit: between April-July for the nesting season
